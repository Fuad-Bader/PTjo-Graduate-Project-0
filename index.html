What is PTjo?

PTjo is a premier Offensive Security Platform designed to bridge the gap between enterprise security needs and world-class cybersecurity talent. Unlike traditional, one-time penetration tests, PTjo provides a dynamic environment for continuous vulnerability discovery.

Continuous Protection

We don't just test once; we monitor your perimeter 24/7 using elite pentesters.

The "Hacker" Perspective

By inviting ethical hackers to test your systems, you see your infrastructure exactly as an attacker would.

Core Mission

How it works

  • 1. Onboarding: Define your scope—be it Network, AD, Cloud, or Mobile.
  • 2. Engagement: Our pre-vetted elite hackers begin deep-dive testing into your assets.
  • 3. Reporting: Real-time vulnerability findings appear on your dashboard with severity levels.
  • 4. Remediation: Collaborate with experts to fix issues and re-test immediately.
