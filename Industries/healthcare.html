Top Security Threats in Healthcare

Our hackers specialize in finding these vulnerabilities

Ransomware Entry Points

Phishing and unpatched systems allow ransomware to encrypt hospital systems and halt patient care.

Critical

IoT Medical Device Exploits

Connected medical devices run outdated firmware vulnerable to remote hijacking and data theft.

Critical

Patient Data Exposure

Misconfigured databases and APIs expose PHI records in violation of HIPAA and GDPR regulations.

Critical

Insecure Remote Access

VPNs and RDP endpoints left exposed are primary entry points for healthcare network breaches.

High

Third-Party Integrations

Medical software vendors with weak security become attack vectors into hospital core systems.

Weak Authentication

Shared credentials and lack of MFA on clinical systems enable unauthorized access to patient records.
