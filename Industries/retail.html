Top Security Threats in Retail

Our hackers specialize in finding these vulnerabilities

Payment Card Skimming

Malicious scripts injected into checkout pages silently capture card data from every transaction.

Critical

Cross-Site Scripting

Stored XSS in product reviews or user profiles can compromise thousands of customer accounts.

High

IDOR in Order Systems

Insecure object references allow attackers to view or modify other customers' orders and addresses.

High

Account Takeover

Credential stuffing and brute force attacks on login endpoints enable mass account takeovers.

High

API Data Leakage

Poorly scoped API responses expose internal pricing, inventory, and customer PII to unauthorized users.

High

SQL Injection

Product search and filter inputs are common injection points that expose entire customer databases.

Critical
